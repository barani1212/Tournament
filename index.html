🏆 Match Format

  • League cum Knockout
  • Top 2 teams from each pool will qualify for Quarterfinals.
  • All matches will be played for 21 points (1 set only).
  • Knockout matches will follow single elimination.
  • Tie in pool? Head-to-head and point difference will be used.

📜 Rules & Regulations

  1. All matches will be played to 21 points – no deuce.
  2. Umpire’s decision is final.
  3. Players must respect opponents, umpire, and club premises.
  4. Use only non-marking shoes on the court.
